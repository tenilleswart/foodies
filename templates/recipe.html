SALMON POKE BOWL

The word poke is hawaian for pieces,poke bowls originated from using fresh caught fish cut into pieces added to a bowl of whatever you want.

Ingredients

Serves 2 people

  • 200g fresh salmon cubed
  • 1 cup of sushi rice
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • avocado chopped
  • seaweed chopped into rectangle sliver
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 lime juiced

Preparation

  • Combined soy sauce,sesame oil,rice vinegar and lime
  • Cube the salmon and pour half the sauce over to marinade
  • Cooked the sushi rice as per packet instructions
  • thinly slice the cucumber into sticks
  • thinly slice the carrot into sticks
  • Combine all ingredients in a bowl and spoon over sauce
  • Serve!
